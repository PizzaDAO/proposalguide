PizzaDAO Proposal Recipe

Here's a recipe for getting some dough from the pizza mafia, plus a list of tips on how to curry favor with the famiglia. Only PizzaDAO NFT holders can submit PizzaDAO proposals, capiche? If you don't have one, you can get one at RarePizzas.com or ask a holder to sponsor your proposal.

Claim Your Discord Role

Follow these 3 steps to connect your wallet and claim your voting role:

  1. 1. Go to the #collabland-join Channel:
    • Open Discord and navigate to the PizzaDAO server.
    • Find the channel named #collabland-join.
  2. 2. Connect Your Wallet:
    • Look for our Mafia Bouncer bot asking you to connect your wallet.
    • Click on the connection prompt or type /connect if available.
    • Choose your wallet (like MetaMask) and confirm the connection on your wallet app.
  3. 3. Get Your Role:
    • After connecting, the bot will check if you own a Rare Pizza Box or Rare Pizza NFT.
    • If you do, you'll automatically get the Box Mafia or Pizza Mafia role.
Note: Make sure you're connecting to the official bot to avoid scams.

      This is very important as we only allow our box and pizza nft holders the ability to vote.
